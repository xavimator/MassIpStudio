/ THE STUDIO

A small team, obsessed with detail, working as an extension of yours.

Mass Ip Studio is a 3D design studio made up of technical artists and art directors. We work with product, architecture, and technology brands that need to visualize something before manufacturing, building, or launching it.

Every project runs through the same hands from start to finish — no middlemen, no team handoffs halfway through.

/ OUR GOAL
From idea
to form.

A product no one can see is a product no one can want.

Many brands fail to show their product in time: prototype photos, generic renders, or nothing at all. That costs sales, investment, and trust.

Our job is to understand the geometry, material, and light of what you're building, and translate it into images that feel real — before they are.

/ HOW WE WORK
GEOMETRY FIRST

Every project starts by validating proportion and structure before thinking about material or light.

VISIBLE ITERATION

We share rough progress — clay renders, wireframes — to decide together before polishing.

LIGHT WITH INTENT

Lighting isn't decoration: it directs the eye and communicates material.

REUSABLE ARCHIVE

We deliver organized assets, ready for production, marketing, or future pieces.
